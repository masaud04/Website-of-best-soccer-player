Sakib Al-Hassan

Khondaker Sakib Al-Hassan; Bengali: খন্দকার সাকিব আল হাসান; born 24 March, 1987 is a Bangladeshi cricketer and current captain of the Bangladesh national cricket team in all formats. He is known for his aggressive left-handed batting style in the middle order and controlled slow left-arm orthodox bowling.

Born: March 24, 1987 (age 36 years), Magura District.
Spouse: Umme Ahmed Shishir (m. 2012)
Current teams: Bangladesh national cricket team (All-rounder)
Parents: Khondoker Masroor Reza, Shirin Reza.
Siblings: Jannatul Ferdous Ritu.

Number of centuries: 14

Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer and the former captain of the Indian national cricket team. Widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of the sport, he plays for Royal Challengers Bangalore in the IPL and Delhi in domestic cricket.

Born: November 5, 1988 (age 34 years), New Delhi, India
Spouse: Anushka Sharma (m. 2017)
Parents: Prem Kohli, Saroj Kohli
Siblings: Vikas Kohli, Bhawna Kohli Dhingra.

Number of centuries: 75
